Founder

I’m the founder of Moda Studio AI—a creative director and systems thinker working at the intersection of merchandising, storytelling and AI. Moda Studio AI is my vision for a new kind of brand house: one that unites taste, narrative and automation to build enduring luxury brands.

Authority

For over a decade I’ve shaped retail experiences and crafted narratives for luxury houses and boutique brands. With expertise spanning merchandising strategy, creative direction and machine learning, I know how to build brand infrastructures that scale without losing soul.
